Bichem Laboratories

Bichem Laboratories, headquartered in Karawal Nagar, Delhi, is a pioneering company dedicated to the manufacturing and marketing of high-quality feed supplements in India and abroad. At BICHEM LABORATORIES, we have braced ourselves for immense growth in all spheres of our operations, including R&D and Manufacturing/Production in GMP conditions. With a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, we are committed to excellence in providing nutritional solutions for the livestock and aquaculture industries.

Our Mission

At Bichem Laboratories, our mission is to enhance the health and productivity of livestock and aquaculture through innovative and scientifically formulated feed supplements. We strive to contribute to the sustainable growth of the agricultural sector by delivering superior products that meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Quality Assurance

At Bichem Laboratories, quality is our top priority. We adhere to stringent quality control measures at every stage of the manufacturing process, from sourcing raw materials to final product testing. Our manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced technology and operated by a team of experienced professionals.

Global Presence

With a strong presence in both domestic and international markets, Bichem Laboratories is committed to serving the needs of customers around the world. We work closely with distributors and partners to ensure timely delivery and personalized support, allowing us to build long-lasting relationships.
