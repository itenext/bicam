About us

Research-led animal healthcare from Bichem Laboratories.

We manufacture and market veterinary and feed supplement solutions designed for practical animal health, productivity, and professional confidence.

Company

Bichem Laboratories

Headquartered in Karawal Nagar, Delhi, Bichem Laboratories is dedicated to manufacturing and marketing high-quality feed supplements in India and abroad. With manufacturing operations in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, the company focuses on quality, research, and responsive animal healthcare support.

Mission

Better animal health, stronger productivity.

Our mission is to enhance the health and productivity of livestock, poultry, pets, and aquaculture through scientifically formulated supplements and veterinary solutions that meet the evolving needs of customers.

Quality

Quality assurance at every stage.

Quality remains central to our process, from sourcing raw materials to final product checks. Our production discipline and experienced professionals support consistent, field-ready outcomes.

Presence

Reliable support for partners and customers.

We work with field partners to make product access, guidance, and delivery more dependable for customers across domestic and international markets.
